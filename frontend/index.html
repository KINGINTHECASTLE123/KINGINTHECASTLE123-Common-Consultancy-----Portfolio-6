Overview

The Russo-Ukrainian war has seen a meaningful impact on lives, economies and geopolitics, causing a ripple effect throughout the entire world. In retrospect, present and foreseeable future, discussions about this war will remain a central part of daily life. In relation to this topic, we have attempted to design a webpage that analyzes public opinion of the ongoing war in Ukraine through visualisations.
